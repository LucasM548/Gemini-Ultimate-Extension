# Privacy Policy for Gemini Ultimate

**Last updated:** December 19, 2025

## Overview

Gemini Ultimate is a browser extension that enhances your experience on Google Gemini. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## Data Storage

This extension uses Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) to save your preferences, such as:
- Custom prompts configuration
- UI settings

**This data is stored entirely on your device** and is never transmitted to any external server.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:
- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is tracked
- No analytics or telemetry data is sent
- No cookies are used for tracking purposes

## Host Permissions

This extension requires access to `gemini.google.com` solely to inject custom functionality into the Gemini interface. No data from these pages is collected or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
